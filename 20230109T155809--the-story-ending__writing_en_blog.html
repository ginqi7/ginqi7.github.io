The Story Ending

On the way home, he suddenly thought it would be better to buy some fruit for his parents, so he found a good-looking fruit stand on the side of the road. He forgot what fruit his parents liked to eat, so he squatted there and chose as wide varieties as possible.

"Boss, how much is this kind of pear per catty?"

The sound was the same as the half-hour recording on the phone. It was a voice he would never be mistaken. He raised his head and saw the side face that had been sketched countless times in his mind; he seemed to be stunned. He just watched quietly, didn't make a sound, and didn't stand up. The woman who bought the pears took the pears, turned around, and was surprised to see the man squatting there looking at her. But it quickly turned into a gentle smile.

"you're back!"

He stood up, straightened his clothes, and then spoke.

"Hey, it's been a long time. I didn't expect you to have such long hair."

"Yeah, ladies still look better with long hair. You've changed a lot too."

"Haha, handsome. Are you starting to regret that you decided to get rid of yourself so early?"

With a joking tone, he unconsciously glanced at the ring on her left ring finger. As if the ring was too dazzling, he quickly looked away.

"Heh, you're still so narcissistic."

Her tone seemed a little awkward, and he realized that there were some jokes that could no longer be played. For a moment, he didn't know what to say. Out of nowhere, a sentence popped up:

"You look so pretty now."

"Thank you." She didn't know what to say to him. Long time no see.

He was very annoyed that he had led the topic into a dead end. Then she noticed that the off-white scarf he was wearing didn't match the clothes at all. Find topical suggestions:

"You still dress so casually, and the scarf doesn't look very nice."

He froze for a moment, stared straight at him, then seemed relieved, and suddenly smiled heartily.

"Yeah! But it's warm."

For a moment, it was as if a hole in his heart had been filled, and the stuck wheel of fate began to spin again. Maybe he has been waiting for this moment for five years, to be able to smile calmly in front of her. In the next moment, he was pushed forward by fate, and he thought he should have the courage to step into the future.
